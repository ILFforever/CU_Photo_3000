# .impeccable.md — Design Context

## Design Context

### Users
~70 university students and staff from CU, TU, and MU attending a joint photography trip to the mountains. They access this on their phones while on the trip — checking schedules, finding their buddy, looking up group info. Context is personal, shared, and time-specific.

### Brand Personality
**Cool · Calm · Mountain**
This is not a product or a company. It is a memory — a weekend in nature with friends from three universities. The interface should feel like a well-designed field notebook or a curated photo zine: quiet, intentional, and warm without being loud.

### Aesthetic Direction
- Dark mode only. Deep blue-green backgrounds — like a forest at night or a mountain after sunset.
- Teal accent, muted — waterfall teal, not neon. Used sparingly.
- Typography does the heavy lifting: large Thai serif for weight, italic Playfair for character.
- Layouts are editorial, not dashboard. Whitespace is generous.
- University colors (CU crimson, TU red, MU navy) appear as subtle accents — identity markers, not paint.
- **Not corporate** — no cards with drop shadows everywhere, no blue primary buttons, no SaaS feel.
- **Not gamer/neon** — no glows, no gradients from purple to pink, no dark mode clichés.

### Design Principles
1. **Stillness over stimulation** — every element earns its place. If it doesn't need to be there, remove it.
2. **Nature palette** — colors evoke mountain, forest, mist. Never artificial or electric.
3. **Thai-first typography** — Thai text is the primary voice; English sits quieter underneath as a label or subtitle.
4. **Honest interactions** — transitions are purposeful and calm (expo/quart easing). Nothing bounces or spins for no reason.
5. **Personal, not public** — this is for 70 people who know each other. The tone is warm and direct, not polished marketing copy.
